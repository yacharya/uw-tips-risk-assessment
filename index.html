Assessing Risk Game

Instructions:

  • You will be presented with 10 “real-life” situational cards that are randomly placed on the top and bottom of the screen. The aim of this activity is to arrange the cards based on your assessment of the level of risk associated with the pictured behavior.

  • Once on the page, drag and drop the cards from “Least Risky” to “Most Risky” -arranging the cards from Least Risky (1-5) on the top row and Most Risky (6-10) on the bottom row.

  • Enjoy!
